The Ministry

Elections

Ministers are elected on the 1st of each month with 1 week of campaigning time before the results are announced. All elections will use Instant-runoff voting.

Term extension

If the number of citizens excluding royalty in the Discord server is less than or equal to 5 and campaigning for the next election is due to start within the next 3 days the Ministry may choose to extend their term by 1 month through a unanimous vote and then royal assent.

Budgets

When the term has been extended, the royalty may choose to increase the budgets of ministers.

Impeachment of ministers

If a minister is not doing their job another minister may start an impeachment against them after royal assent if all non-involved ministers agree a vote is sent to the general population if there is a majority, that minister is removed and the candidate from the last election with the next highest vote will fill the spot.

Royal prerogative

If the royalty agrees that a minister is doing more harm than good or has committed misconduct that minister may be removed without a vote.

Reimbursement

If a minister keeps good financial records including: what they purchased, how much it cost, rationale, where/who they bought it from, date, approximate time and proof of purchase, they may apply for reimbursement up to their budget for the term during campaigning for the next term or within 24 hours after resignation, each minister may only request reimbursement once per term. A minister's budget is decided at the beginning of their term.

Loans

If a minister can't afford to do their duties they may apply for a loan when running for ministry and after the term has been extended until the end of the month, loans will be granted on the first day of a month or when a member of royalty is able to join EarthMC after the first of the month; this loan must be paid back during the campaigning period for the next term or within 24 hours after resigning. If a minister can't afford to buy something that they need to perform their duties, they may ask a member of the royalty to give them a small loan out of the nation's bank to make a single purchase, this may be done as often as the minister wants as long as there is enough room in their budget. Reimbursement will be subtracted from what a minister owes from a ministerial loan if they have a loan.

Lack of ministers

In the event that not enough people run in an election or a minister is impeached with no candidate to replace them their duty will be fulfilled by the royalty.

Multiple positions

A minister may only hold one position however they may run for as many as they want. If a minister wins multiple positions they will have to pick one and the other/s will goto the next candidate or the royalty as defined in Sections 2.2 and 2.4.

Resignation

If a minister no longer wants to be a minister they may resign by making an announcement in #private-announcements. When a minister resigns their position will goto the next candidate or the royalty as defined in Sections 2.2 and 2.4.

Ministerial Positions

Minister of Defence

The minister who holds this position is responsible for defending the citizens of Sudan, enlisting, training and supplying the military.

Minister of Foreign Affairs

The minister who holds this position is responsible for keeping peace with other nations and making the nation well known(for good reasons).

Minister of Internal Affairs and Expansion

The minister who holds this position is responsible for making sure that citizens are happy, no civil wars happen and getting new towns to join the nation or be created in the nation.

Confidentiality of governmental proceedings

All governmental proceedings shall be visible to (not all) citizens and allies unless a vote passes to have it in private.

States of emergencies

In the event that quick decision making is essential in an emergency, the ministry may have a vote to give the royalty temporary power to make temporary decisions.

Royal assent

Any legal process may require Royal Assent. If a process requires Royal Assent, the Royalty must agree to it for it to happen. The royalty are to give a reason for any denials of Royal Assent.

Changing this document

This document may only be changed through a referendum(a vote that all citizens may partake in) with a 75% majority. Referendums require royal assent.
